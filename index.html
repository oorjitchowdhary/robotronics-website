About Us

Tech Syndicate is the prestigious tech club of Amity International School, Sector 46, Gurgaon.

Since its inception in 2010, the club is constantly working to make technology accessible to everyone and spark an interest in the youth to keep up with the latest developments in the field of computer science.

Our club's motive is to use technology creatively to solve real world problems. Robotronics, our tech symposium, was first hosted in 2013. This contest has truly been a challenge for all the tech wizards from Delhi NCR.

As a legacy we'd like to continue, we're hosting inTech'19 -- an intra-school event simulating the actual Robotronics for providing the students a platform to showcase their talent and creativity in the field of information technology.

prod.io (Audio/Visual Editing)

1 Team of 2 Members per School
Eligibility: IX-XII

Prelims topic will be released on our Facebook page
Software: any video editing software

An .mp4 file is to be uploaded on Google Drive or YouTube and the link is to be sent to cyberamity46@gmail.com with school name and video details (if any) in a readme.txt file for the event by 11:59pm 10th July 2019.

The duration of the video should be 3-5 minutes and the video should have a story.
The video will be assessed on the basis of editing, creativity, videography and story.

Selected teams will be informed via mail and the results will be uploaded on our Facebook page by 15th July 2019.

Weev (Web Development)

1 Team of 2 Members per School
Eligibility: IX-XII

Prelims topic will be released on our Facebook page
Languages: HTML, CSS, Javascript, jQuery

Use of Font Awesome icons and online jQuery libraries is allowed but original graphics will be given bonus points.

All files are to be compressed in a .rar file and mailed to cyberamity46@gmail.com with school name and the details in a README file for the event by 11:59pm 10th July 2019.

The video will be assessed on the basis of navigation & functionality, originality/innovation, aesthestic and features.

Selected teams will be informed via mail and the results will be uploaded on our Facebook page by 15th July 2019.
